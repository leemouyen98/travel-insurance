Common Questions

Questions Clients Often Ask

Who can apply?

Available to Malaysians and non-Malaysians departing from Malaysia. Domestic plan can also apply for inbound travel within Malaysia.

What is the age limit?

Individual and group eligibility goes up to age 85. Family plan eligibility goes up to age 70.

What is the difference between domestic and international?

Domestic is for travel within Malaysia. International uses Area 1, Area 2, or Area 3 depending on destination.

What is CFAR?

Cancellation for Any Reason is available on Deluxe only, subject to policy wording and claim conditions.

Which plans get Flight Delay Pass?

Essential and Deluxe.

How does the 5% group discount work?

It applies to group policies for 2 or more people travelling together.
